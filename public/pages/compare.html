Vulnerability Info

Server-Side Request Forgery (SSRF) occurs when an attacker can influence a server-side application to make HTTP requests to an arbitrary domain of their choosing.

In this sandbox, the product fetcher doesn't validate the destination. You can use it to "pivot" and scan internal network resources that are normally shielded from the public internet.

Risk: Accessing sensitive credentials from cloud metadata services or mapping internal environment interfaces.
